Work Examples

Zombie Simulation (C++)

This is an Eco-Simulation, you can consider it to be a basic verson of Jonhn Conways "The Game Of Life". This was the final assignment in our C++ course, and it was one of my favourites, I felt that it tought me a lot of concepts, such as path-finding, 2D-Arrays and working with pointers. It also taught be a deeper level of fundamental concepts of OOP, such as Inheritence, Abstraction and this assignment had a heavy focus on Polymorphism, so with this assignment I not only got a better understanding of C++ but I also gained a deeper understanding of Polymorphism
